About Us

Aloha Juice Bar

Aloha Juice Bar is known for its healthy products that don't sacrifice on flavor. It's locally owned and operated, specializing in pressed juices, smoothies and bowls. With a focus on quality ingredients and unique flavors, The Aloha Juice Bar serves up nothing but the best.
